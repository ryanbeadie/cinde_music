C_indie Music is a free open source webpage designed to help fans find the music! Here you can browse reviews and info about Twin Cities artists and venues. All has been provided by you the fan! If you know of a musician or a place to see music and its not listed....add it to the list!! Keep in mind art is and always will be "in the eye of the beholder". We here at C_indie Music implore you to be real without being unfavorable or adverse. Like Craig Finn says..."Stay Positive!!"
